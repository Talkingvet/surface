Creates a Task in Zoho CRM attached to this contact. Completing it in Surface marks it Completed in Zoho; edits to title, date or notes stay in step.

How it works

  • Off by default. The editor is unchanged until you flip the toggle — and the toggle only exists for people who've connected Zoho in Settings.
  • Contact search is live. Type a few letters and pick from your real CRM contacts — nothing to maintain in Surface.
  • Owner defaults to you. Assigning a teammate's CRM task is one click; they see it in Zoho as usual.
  • One save, two systems. The task lands in your Surface list and in Zoho simultaneously, and checking it off closes both.

What the team sees

  • Tasks logged to the CRM wear a quiet CRM chip in every view — you always know what's on the record.
  • Surface-only tasks stay exactly as they are today.
